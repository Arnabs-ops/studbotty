Flashcards: the process of respiration

1. What is the primary function of respiration?
To convert glucose into energy for the body.
2. Which process generates carbon dioxide as a byproduct?
Exhalation
3. Where does cellular respiration primarily take place?
Within the mitochondria of cells.
4. What are the three main stages of cellular respiration?
Glycolysis, Krebs Cycle, Electron Transport Chain
5. Which gas is produced as a waste product during respiration?
Carbon dioxide
