Flashcards: photosynthesis

1. What is photosynthesis?
The process plants use to convert light energy into chemical energy (sugar).
2. What are the two main stages of photosynthesis?
Light-dependent reactions and the Calvin cycle.
3. Where does photosynthesis primarily occur?
In plant leaves, specifically within chloroplasts.
4. What are the primary products of photosynthesis?
Glucose (sugar) and oxygen.
5. Why is photosynthesis important for life on Earth?
It produces oxygen, which is essential for many organisms, and provides the base of most food chains.
